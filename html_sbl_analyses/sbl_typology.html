Geographic Distribution of the Short-Before-Long Principle

These maps visualize various Short-Before-Long measures across the globe. The standard color scale indicates compliance with the Short-Before-Long principle:

  • Green: Strong compliance (Score > 0.1)
  • Yellow: Weak or neutral compliance (0 ≤ Score ≤ 0.1)
  • Red: Inverse tendency (Long-Before-Short) (Score < 0)

1. Right SbL β Slope

Compliance with Short-Before-Long on the Right side.

2. Left SbL β Slope (Inverted)

Compliance with Short-Before-Long on the Left side. Left slopes are negated so Green means compliant.

3. Joint Average SbL β

The average of the Right and (inverted) Left SbL β slopes. Green indicates overall bilateral compliance.

4. Right Horizontal Law Strict Compliance

Percentage of verb dependencies that strictly obey the Right Horizontal Law. Mapped to the color scale such that >60% is Green, and <50% is Red.
